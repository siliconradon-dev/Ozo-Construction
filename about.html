ABOUT OZO CONSTRUCTIONS

The company was established in November 2014 as a Construction Company for Commercial Buildings. For the last 9 years, OZO Construction (Pvt) Ltd stood for its quality, integrity, and professionalism. We are dedicated to superior workmanship and adhere strictly to the budget, schedule, and service commitments. The company was registered on 03rd August 2021.
